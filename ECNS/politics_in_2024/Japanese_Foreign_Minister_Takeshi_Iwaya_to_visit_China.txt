Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Takeshi Iwaya to visit China
Date: Dec 24, 2024

Japanese Foreign Minister Takeshi Iwaya is invited to visit China on December 25, the Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Mao Ning announced on Tuesday.
Chinese and Japanese foreign ministers will hold talks and attend the second meeting of High-Level Consultation Mechanism on People-to-People and Cultural Exchanges between China and Japan, according to the spokesperson.
