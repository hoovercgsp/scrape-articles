Title: Xi says China-Kyrgyzstan-Uzbekistan railway project strategic decision to regional connectivity, prosperity
Date: Dec 27, 2024

Chinese President Xi Jinping said Friday the construction of the China-Kyrgyzstan-Uzbekistan railway is a strategic decision made by the three governments to promote regional connectivity, prosperity and stability.
Xi made the remarks in his congratulatory letter to the commencement ceremony of the railway project.
