TEHRAN, Feb. 4 (Xinhua) -- Iran's non-oil exports in the first 10 months of the current Iranian calendar year rose by 18 percent over the same period last year, the country's Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) said on Monday.
Non-oil exports were worth 47.75 billion U.S. dollars in the first 10 months of the current Iranian calendar year, which starts on March 20, 2024. The total weight of non-oil exports reached 127.39 million tons, a 12-percent increase, according to TPO figures.
Iran's major non-oil exports included natural gas, liquefied propane, methanol, liquefied butane and pitch, with the main export destinations being China, Iraq, the United Arab Emirates and Türkiye, said the TPO. ■
