BAGHDAD, Feb. 2 (Xinhua) -- An "unknown" attack targeted the Khor Mor gas field in the province of Sulaymaniyah in northern Iraq's semi-autonomous region of Kurdistan on Sunday evening, a provincial security source said.
The attack occurred around 7:00 p.m. local time (1600 GMT) when two explosions believed to be caused by a drone or rocket hit the Khor Mor gas field, the source told Xinhua on condition of anonymity.
The source added there are no immediate reports of human casualties or damage.
