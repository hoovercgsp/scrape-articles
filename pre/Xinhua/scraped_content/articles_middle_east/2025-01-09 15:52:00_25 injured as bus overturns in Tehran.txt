TEHRAN, Jan. 9 (Xinhua) -- Twenty-five people have been injured as a bus overturned on a highway south of Tehran after colliding with a Jersey barrier on Wednesday, the official news agency IRNA reported.
The accident was reported at 19:44 local time (1614 GMT), with 31 people on board the overturned bus, IRNA quoted Jalal Maleki, spokesman of the Tehran fire department, as saying.
Rescue teams, including firefighters and emergency medical personnel, were immediately sent to the scene.
