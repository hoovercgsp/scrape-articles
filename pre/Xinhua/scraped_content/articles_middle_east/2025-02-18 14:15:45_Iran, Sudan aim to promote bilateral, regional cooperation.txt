TEHRAN, Feb. 18 (Xinhua) -- Iran and Sudan on Monday signed two memorandums of understanding (MoUs), aiming to enhance cooperation at both bilateral and regional levels, the official news agency IRNA reported.
The MoUs were signed by Iranian Foreign Minister Seyed Abbas Araghchi and his Sudanese counterpart Ali Yousif Ahmed.
