TEHRAN, Jan. 31 (Xinhua) -- The 43rd Fajr Film Festival opened in the Iranian capital Tehran Thursday evening.
The opening ceremony featured guests such as Iranian Culture Minister Seyyed Abbas Salehi, President of the Iranian Organization of Cinema and Audiovisual Affairs Raed Faridzadeh, as well as the country's artists and filmmakers.
According to the festival's official website, 33 Iranian features will participate in the main competition section of the event, which lasts from Friday to Feb. 10.
This year, the festival's international section will be held separately, and the date will be announced soon.
