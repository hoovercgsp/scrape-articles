RIYADH, Feb. 12 (Xinhua) -- "Souk Wonders" is an immersive indoor market experience that is part of Diriyah Season, an annual entertainment and cultural festival, in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
The event, running from Feb. 6 to Feb. 28, reimagines the traditional marketplace with a theatrical twist. Visitors can enjoy a fusion of culinary delights, art, and live performances within a vibrant indoor setting.
