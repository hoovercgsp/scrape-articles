JERUSALEM, Feb. 8 (Xinhua) -- Israeli fighter jets on Saturday conducted an intelligence-based strike on a Hamas weapons storage facility in the southern Syrian town of Deir Ali, the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) said in a statement.
It added that the weapons stored at the facility were intended for use in attacks on IDF troops.
