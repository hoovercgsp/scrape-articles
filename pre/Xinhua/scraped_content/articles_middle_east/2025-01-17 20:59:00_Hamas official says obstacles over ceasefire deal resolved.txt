GAZA, Jan. 17 (Xinhua) -- A Hamas official said on Friday that obstacles with Israel over a ceasefire deal have been resolved.
"The mediators have succeeded in solving the obstacles created by Israel. We sought a national exchange deal from all factions and our people," Zahir Jabareen, the senior Hamas official, said in a press statement.
He added that the list of the prisoners who will be released in the first phase of the swap deal will be announced later.
