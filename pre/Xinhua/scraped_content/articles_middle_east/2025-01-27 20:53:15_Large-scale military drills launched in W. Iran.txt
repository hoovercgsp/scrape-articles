TEHRAN, Jan. 27 (Xinhua) -- The Iranian army's ground force launched large-scale "security, offensive and mobile" drills in the western province of Kermanshah on Monday, the semi-official Tasnim news agency reported.
The drills, involving the ground force's special force, airborne and armored brigades, aviation and drone units, and electronic warfare battalions, started in the Naft Shahr region of Qasr-e Shirin County, according to the report.
The drills aim to improve the forces' preparedness to counter any foreign threat to Iran's order and security, according to Tasnim.
