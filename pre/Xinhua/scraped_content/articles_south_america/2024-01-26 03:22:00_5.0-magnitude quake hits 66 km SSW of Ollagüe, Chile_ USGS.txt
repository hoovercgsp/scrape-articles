NEW YORK, Jan. 25 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.0 jolted 66 km SSW of Ollagüe, Chile at 18:58:12 GMT on Thursday, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
The epicenter, with a depth of 116.1 km, was initially determined to be at 21.79 degrees south latitude and 68.46 degrees west longitude. ■
