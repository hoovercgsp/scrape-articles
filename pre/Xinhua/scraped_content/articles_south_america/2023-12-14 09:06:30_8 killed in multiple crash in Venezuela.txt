CARACAS, Dec. 13 (Xinhua) -- At least eight people were killed and 14 others injured in a multiple crash on the highway connecting the Venezuelan capital of Caracas with the coastal city of Guarenas, authorities confirmed Wednesday.
The multiple collision involved 17 vehicles and led to a fire, Vice Minister for Risk Management and Civil Protection Carlos Perez Ampueda said on X, formerly Twitter.
The incident was caused by the collision of a lorry with vehicles that were there due to a previous incident, the official said.
