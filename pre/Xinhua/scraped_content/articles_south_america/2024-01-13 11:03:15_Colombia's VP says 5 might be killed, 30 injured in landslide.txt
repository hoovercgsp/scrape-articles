BOGOTA, Jan. 12 (Xinhua) -- Colombia's Vice President Francia Marquez said Friday that a strong landslide in northwestern Colombia might have left five dead and 30 wounded.
A landslide on the Quibdo-Medellin highway has left dozens of people and vehicles trapped in the area, Marquez said on X, formerly Twitter.
