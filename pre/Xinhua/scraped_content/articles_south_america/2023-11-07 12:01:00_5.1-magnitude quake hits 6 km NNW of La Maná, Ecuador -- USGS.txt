BEIJING, Nov. 7 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.1 jolted 6 km NNW of La Maná, Ecuador at 0333 GMT on Tuesday, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
The epicenter, with a depth of 16.1 km, was initially determined to be at 0.89 degrees south latitude and 79.24 degrees west longitude. ■
