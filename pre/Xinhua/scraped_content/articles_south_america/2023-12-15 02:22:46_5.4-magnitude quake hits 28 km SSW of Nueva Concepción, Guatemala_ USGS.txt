NEW YORK, Dec. 14 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.4 jolted 28 km SSW of Nueva Concepción, Guatemala, at 17:55:01 GMT on Thursday, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
The epicenter, with a depth of 78.2 km, was initially determined to be at 13.96 degrees north latitude and 91.39 degrees west longitude. ■
