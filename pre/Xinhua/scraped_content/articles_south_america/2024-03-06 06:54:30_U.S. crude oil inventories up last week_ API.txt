HOUSTON, March 5 (Xinhua) -- The American Petroleum Institute (API) on Tuesday reported an increase of 0.423 million barrels of crude oil in U.S. inventories for the week ending March 1.
Analysts expected a rise of 2.6 million barrels for the week.
The API reported a hike of 8.428 million barrels in the previous week.
Oil prices fell on Tuesday.
