RIO DE JANEIRO, Feb. 16 (Xinhua) -- Brazil recorded an unemployment rate of 7.8 percent in 2023, down 1.8 percentage points from 2022, the state-run Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics (IBGE) reported on Friday.
The unemployed population in 2023 was 8.5 million, 1.8 million less than in 2022, while the employed population hit a record of 100.7 million, up 3.8 percent over 2022.
