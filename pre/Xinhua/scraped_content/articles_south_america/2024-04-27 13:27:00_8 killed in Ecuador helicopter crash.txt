QUITO, April 26 (Xinhua) -- Five soldiers and three civilians were killed in an Ecuadorian army helicopter crash on Friday, the armed forces said.
The helicopter, which was carrying humanitarian aid to the people affected by floods, crashed at 9:36 a.m. (1436 GMT) in the Tiwino district of Pastaza Province, said the Ecuadorian army in a statement.
