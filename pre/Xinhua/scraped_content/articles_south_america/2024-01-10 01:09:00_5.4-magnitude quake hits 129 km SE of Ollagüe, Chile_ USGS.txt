NEW YORK, Jan. 9 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.4 jolted 129 km SE of Ollagüe, Chile, at 16:47:25 GMT on Tuesday, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
The epicenter, with a depth of 179.4 km, was initially determined to be at 21.92 degrees south latitude and 67.25 degrees west longitude. ■
