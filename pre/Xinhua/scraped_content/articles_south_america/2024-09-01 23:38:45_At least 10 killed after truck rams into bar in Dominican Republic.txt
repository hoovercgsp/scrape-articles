PANAMA CITY, Sept. 1 (Xinhua) -- At least 10 people were killed and 17 injured after a truck crashed into a bar in the western Dominican Republic on Sunday morning, local media reported.
The accident occurred around 3 a.m. (0700 GMT) in the city of Las Yayas in the province of Azua, when a truck went out of control at high speed and crashed into a local bar, causing many casualties, according to local media reports.
Local traffic authorities said the death toll was likely to rise as some of the injured were in serious condition.
