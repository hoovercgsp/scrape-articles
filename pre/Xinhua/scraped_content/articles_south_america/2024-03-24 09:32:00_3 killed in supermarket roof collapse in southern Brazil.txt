SAO PAULO, March 23 (Xinhua) -- The roof of a supermarket collapsed Friday night in the southern Brazilian city of Pontal do Parana, killing three people and injuring 10 others, the local fire department reported Saturday.
The tragedy happened at the Super Rede Atacadista supermarket where hundreds of people were attending its opening party.
The water tanks of the supermarket gave way and caused the roof to collapse, killing a woman instantly.
