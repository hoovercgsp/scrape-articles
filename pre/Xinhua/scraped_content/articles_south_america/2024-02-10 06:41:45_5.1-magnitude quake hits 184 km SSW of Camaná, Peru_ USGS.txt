NEW YORK, Feb. 9 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.1 jolted 184 km SSW of Camaná, Peru at 22:17:03 GMT on Friday, the U.S. Geological Survey said.
The epicenter, with a depth of 10.0 km, was initially determined to be at 18.11 degrees south latitude and 73.51 degrees west longitude. ■
