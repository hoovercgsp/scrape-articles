LA PAZ, July 1 (Xinhua) -- The Bolivian government summoned the Argentine ambassador to Bolivia, Marcelo Adrian Massoni, to express its "strong rejection of the statements made by the Office of the President of the Republic of Argentina, Javier Milei," regarding the failed coup d'état against President Luis Arce that occurred on June 26, the Bolivian Foreign Ministry reported on Monday. ■
