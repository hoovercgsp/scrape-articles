RIO DE JANEIRO, Dec. 23 (Xinhua) -- Five people, including a minor, died Saturday when a plane crashed in a residential area in the Brazilian state of Sao Paulo, local media reported.
The firefighters did not reveal whether all the victims were traveling on the plane, which was "small in size," said the reports.
