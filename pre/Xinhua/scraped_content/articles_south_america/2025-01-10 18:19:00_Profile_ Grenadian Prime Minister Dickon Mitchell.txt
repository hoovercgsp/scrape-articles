BEIJING, Jan. 10 (Xinhua) -- At the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Qiang, Grenadian Prime Minister Dickon Mitchell will pay an official visit to China from Jan. 11 to 17.
Dickon Mitchell, 47, was elected leader of the National Democratic Congress of Grenada in October 2021. He was sworn in as prime minister in June 2022.
