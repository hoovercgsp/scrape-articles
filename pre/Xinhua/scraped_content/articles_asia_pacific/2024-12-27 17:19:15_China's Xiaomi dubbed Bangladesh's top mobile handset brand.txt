DHAKA, Dec. 27 (Xinhua) -- Chinese smartphone maker Xiaomi has been awarded the prestigious title of Most Loved Brand in the Mobile Handset category by Bangladesh Brand Forum during the 16th edition of the Best Brand Award 2024.
The Best Brand Award, a flagship initiative of the Bangladesh Brand Forum, has been honoring the nation's most loved and impactful brands for the past 15 years.
The award was presented to Ziauddin Chowdhury, country manager of Xiaomi Bangladesh, at a grand gala ceremony held in Dhaka Thursday night.
