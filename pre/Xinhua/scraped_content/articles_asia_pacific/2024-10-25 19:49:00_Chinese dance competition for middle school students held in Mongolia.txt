ULAN BATOR, Oct. 25 (Xinhua) -- The eleventh edition of the Chinese dance competition for middle school students was held in Ulan Bator, the capital of Mongolia, on Friday.
The event, co-organized by the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia and the Confucius Institute at the National University of Mongolia, aimed to strengthen cultural ties between China and Mongolia.
A total of 256 students from 16 schools across Mongolia participated, showcasing 42 dance performances.
