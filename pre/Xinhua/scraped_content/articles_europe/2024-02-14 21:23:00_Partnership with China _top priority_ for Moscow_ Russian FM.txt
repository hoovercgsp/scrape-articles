MOSCOW, Feb. 14 (Xinhua) -- Deepening the partnership with China is a top priority for Moscow, Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov said here Wednesday.
"We will view this as a top priority," Lavrov said while addressing the Russian State Duma, or the lower house of the parliament.
"Our partnership plays a stabilizing role in global affairs," Lavrov said, adding that bilateral trade between the two countries is growing at an impressive rate.
