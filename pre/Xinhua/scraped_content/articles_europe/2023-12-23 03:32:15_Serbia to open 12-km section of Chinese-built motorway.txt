BELGRADE, Dec. 22 (Xinhua) -- A 12-km stretch of the Chinese-built motorway connecting the cities of Lajkovac and Valjevo in western Serbia is scheduled to be opened to traffic this Saturday, Serbia's President Aleksandar Vucic said during his visit to the construction site on Friday.
Accompanied by Minister of Construction, Transport and Infrastructure Goran Vesic, Vucic commended the progress made by the Chinese Shandong Hi-Speed Group and confirmed the opening of the Lajkovac-Divci section of the motorway.
The remaining 5.2-km stretch of the motorway is scheduled to be completed in 2024, he said.
