KAZAN, Russia, Oct. 20 (Xinhua) -- A photo exhibition marking the 75th anniversary of diplomatic ties between China and Russia opened Saturday in the Russian city of Kazan.
The exhibition, held at the National Library of the Republic of Tatarstan, features around 36 photographs from Xinhua News Agency, showcasing important achievements in cooperation between the two countries over the last 75 years.
The exhibition is organized by the Xinhua News Agency Eurasia Regional Bureau and the Union of Chinese Entrepreneurs in Russia in partnership with the Torgmoll company.
