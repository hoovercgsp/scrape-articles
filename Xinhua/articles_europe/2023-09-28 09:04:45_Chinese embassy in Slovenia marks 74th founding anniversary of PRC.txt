LJUBLJANA, Sept. 27 (Xinhua) -- Approximately 300 people attended a reception held here on Wednesday by the Chinese Embassy in Slovenia, celebrating the 74th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China (PRC) and the traditional Chinese Mid-Autumn Festival.
The event was hosted by Chinese Ambassador to Slovenia Wang Shunqing, who greeted overseas Chinese in the country as well as a large number of Slovenian officials, businessmen and academics.
Over the past 74 years, China has achieved remarkable development, Wang said in his speech, adding that as a major powerhouse of global economy growth, China will continue to uphold multilateralism and work with countries around the world to jointly address global challenges.
