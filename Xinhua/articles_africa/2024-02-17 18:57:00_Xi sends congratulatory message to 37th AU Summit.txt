BEIJING, Feb. 17 (Xinhua) -- Chinese President Xi Jinping sent a congratulatory message on Saturday to the opening of the 37th African Union (AU) Summit.
Xi pointed out that in the past year, the China-Africa relationship has kept deepening and developing, noting that China-Africa Leaders' Dialogue was successfully held and both sides have decided to support each other in their respective pursuit of modernization paths, so as to jointly create a good environment for realizing the vision of development.
