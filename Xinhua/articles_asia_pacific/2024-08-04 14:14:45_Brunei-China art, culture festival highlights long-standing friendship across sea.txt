BANDAR SERI BEGAWAN, Aug. 4 (Xinhua) -- The 2nd Brunei-China Art and Visual Culture Festival opened in Brunei on Saturday, highlighting the long-standing friendship and history of cultural exchanges between the two countries.
The festival, featuring traditional martial arts, opera, calligraphy, exhibitions, lectures and more, attracted over 200 guests, including artists, scholars, officials and diplomats.
"China and Brunei are close neighbors across the sea, enjoying a long history of cultural exchanges and friendship. Historical records indicate that the people-to-people contacts between our two countries dated back to more than 1,000 years ago," Chinese Ambassador to Brunei Xiao Jianguo said.
The 2nd Brunei-China Art and Visual Culture Festival was jointly organized by the Chinese Embassy in Brunei, the foreign office of the Nanjing municipal government in China's Jiangsu Province, and the Ministry of Culture, Youth and Sports of Brunei.
