ULAN BATOR, May 30 (Xinhua) -- An event promoting China's tea culture and tourism was held on Thursday at the Chinese Cultural Center in Mongolia's capital of Ulan Bator.
Titled "Tea for Harmony: Yaji Cultural Salon," the event features tea culture demonstrations and an exhibition of a variety of teas, tea sets, and photos showcasing China's tea culture and tourist destinations.
