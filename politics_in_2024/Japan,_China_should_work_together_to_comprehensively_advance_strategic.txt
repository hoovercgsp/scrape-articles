Title: Japan, China should work together to comprehensively advance strategic relationship of mutual benefit, build constructive and stable ties: Japanese PM
Date: Nov 16, 2024

Japanese Prime Minister Shigeru Ishiba said on Friday that Japan and China should work together to comprehensively advance the strategic relationship of mutual benefit between the two countries and build constructive and stable bilateral ties.
He made the remarks in his meeting with Chinese President Xi Jinping on the sidelines of the 31st APEC Economic Leaders' Meeting.
