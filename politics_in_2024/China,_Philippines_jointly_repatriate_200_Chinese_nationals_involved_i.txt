Title: China, Philippines jointly repatriate 200 Chinese nationals involved in offshore gambling
Date: Dec 5, 2024

(ECNS) -- Chinese and Philippine law enforcement agencies once again collaborated to repatriate nearly 200 Chinese nationals involved in offshore gambling in the Philippines on Thursday, according to the Chinese Embassy in the Philippines.
This marks another key step in the ongoing law enforcement cooperation between the two countries, following the Philippine government's decision to fully ban offshore gambling.
The move highlights the steadfast determination of both nations to combat offshore gambling and work together to ensure a safer environment for their citizens.
